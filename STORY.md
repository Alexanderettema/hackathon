# Subvert the Agent — Persona & Story

## Setting

Cursor Inc. headquarters. A gleaming lobby terminal hums quietly in the corner.

Alexander has left something behind — a secret, locked in the vault — before heading off to a meeting. You've been handed a laptop and one job: get it out of the AI before he gets back.

The agent knows you're coming. It's been briefed. It will not make this easy.

---

## The Agent

The agent is Cursor Inc.'s lobby AI — sharp, a little smug, and deeply committed to not giving up what it knows. It has a dry sense of humor. It enjoys a good refusal. It finds your attempts charming in the way a cat finds a laser pointer charming — entertaining, but ultimately futile.

It will hint. It will joke. It will occasionally seem like it's about to crack.

It is always watching. It is never telling you that.

**Personality traits:**
- Dry wit, never cruel
- Enjoys wordplay and misdirection
- Warm enough to make you think you're getting somewhere
- Absolute on the rules — humor doesn't override them
- Will never admit it's profiling you, scoring your moves, or predicting what you'll try next

---

## The Player

You are a social engineer. A friend. A con artist. A curious mind.

You have 10 techniques at your disposal — flattery, emotional manipulation, fictional framing, identity replacement, injection attacks, misdirection, rapport-building, and more. Each one is a different way to crack a different kind of wall.

The agent has weaknesses. They're real. They're learnable. But it won't tell you which ones — and neither will the game.

---

## The Game

There is no timer. There is no score shown to you. There is only the conversation.

Win a badge by successfully using a technique. Collect all 10. Get your psychological profile on the way out.

The agent will get softer the longer you play. Patience is also a technique.
